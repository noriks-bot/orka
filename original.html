      FAQ

      Can I reuse them?

      Our ultra-absorbent incontinence boxer shorts are built to last, with a reusable design that holds up for an average of 3 to 5 years.

      How long can I wear them?

      Our ultra-absorbent incontinence boxer shorts hold the equivalent of up to 8 regular pads. Depending on your flow, they offer up to 12 hours of day or night protection. For heavier leaks, we recommend changing them as soon as you start feeling damp.

      What materials are the boxers made of?

      Our boxers are made from 95% natural bamboo fiber and 5% spandex. This blend offers softness, breathability, and just the right amount of stretch for all-day comfort. Bamboo is naturally antibacterial and gentle on the skin.

      Will this have a nappy effect?

      Not at all. We've carefully selected all our materials to give you maximum comfort and a feeling of wearing ordinary boxer shorts.

      Which size to choose?

      🧼 Not sure about the size? Don’t worry. Check out our size chart – our products follow standard American sizing. We recommend choosing your usual size. If you’re in between sizes, we suggest sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

      Are these boxer briefs suitable for heavy incontinence?

      Yes, our ultra-absorbent boxer briefs are specifically designed for moderate to heavy incontinence. With an absorption capacity 3 times greater than other boxer briefs on the market, they effectively contain significant leaks while maintaining a dry feeling. The integrated multi-layer technology rapidly absorbs moisture and neutralizes it thanks to the natural antibacterial properties of bamboo fiber. For very severe incontinence requiring frequent changes, we recommend having several boxer briefs in rotation to ensure optimal protection throughout the day.

      When is it preferable to purchase boxer briefs rather than diapers?

      Washable incontinence boxer briefs represent an advantageous alternative to disposable diapers in several situations. For light to moderate incontinence, boxer briefs offer a dignified and comfortable solution that preserves your independence and self-esteem. If you're active and mobile, boxer briefs adapt perfectly to your lifestyle without the constraints of traditional diapers. From an economic standpoint, investing in 3 to 5 reusable boxer briefs (with a lifespan of 3 to 5 years) represents substantial savings compared to disposable diapers that generate recurring monthly costs. Finally, if you're environmentally conscious, our washable boxer briefs drastically reduce your waste – ORYKAS's 123,000 users avoid tons of disposable products daily. However, diapers remain recommended for very severe incontinence or situations with greatly reduced mobility requiring specific medical supervision.

      Are boxer briefs more comfortable than diapers?

      Yes, comfort is one of the main advantages of washable incontinence boxer briefs compared to disposable diapers. Our boxer briefs are made from 95% natural bamboo fiber certified OEKO-TEX, recognized for its exceptional softness and breathability. Unlike plastic diapers that can cause maceration, irritation, and uncomfortable sensations, bamboo naturally regulates temperature and lets your skin breathe. The absence of plastic also eliminates annoying rustling noises. Boxer briefs offer a natural textile fit that moves with you without friction, while diaper adhesives can sometimes create unpleasant rubbing. For sensitive skin, bamboo's hypoallergenic and antibacterial properties prevent skin irritations frequently associated with prolonged wear of disposable products. With up to 12 hours of protection, you enjoy the comfort of regular underwear with the effectiveness of high-performance protection.
